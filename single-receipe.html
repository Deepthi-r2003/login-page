Simple velvet cake

A red velvet cake is a combination of a vanilla and chocolate cake, with a distinct tangy taste. This is due to the buttermilk and vinegar which are common ingredients in a red velvet cake.

cooking time : 25mins

Preparation time : 20mins

Serving-time : 4mins

Steps

Step : 1

Gather Your Ingredients.Prepare the Cake Pans.

Step : 2

Measure and Sift Dry Ingredients and Combine Wet Ingredients..

Step : 3

Gradually Add Dry Ingredients.Pour Batter into pan and bake in Preheated Oven.
